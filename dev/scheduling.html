Jobs land in your inbox, Ava lines them up — you just say who and when.

How this works
① A customer emails about a job — Ava spots it and puts it on this board (tagged job / quote-first / not-a-job).
② You hit Assign, pick a tradie and a time — Ava asks them in your team Slack. They just reply 👍 or 👎 in the chat.
③ They say yes → Ava books it in Fergus and emails the customer to confirm. Says no → it comes straight back to you for a new time or tradie.
Works from here or entirely from Slack — your crew never needs this dashboard.
