Privacy Policy

Elastic Admin · Aotearoa New Zealand · Effective 17 July 2026

Elastic Admin ("Elastic", "we") provides bookkeeping and back-office services for trade businesses. Our application — Ava, the Elastic Admin assistant — helps prepare your business's books by gathering the paperwork (receipts, tax invoices and bills) that belongs with your bank transactions. This policy explains what data the application accesses, how it is used, stored and shared, and how to have it deleted.

What we access

  • Google user data (Gmail): with your explicit permission via Google sign-in, the application can read messages and attachments in the Gmail inboxes you choose to connect, and send email on your behalf from those inboxes. The application never modifies, labels or deletes your mail.
  • Accounting data (Xero): with your permission, bank transaction details, invoices and attachments in your Xero organisation.
  • Documents you give us directly: receipts, statements and reports you upload into the app or share with us in your team's messaging channels.

What we use it for

Reading: identifying financial documents (receipts, tax invoices, bills and statements) and extracting the details on them — supplier, date, amount, tax — so each document can be filed against the matching bank transaction and your books can be prepared for reconciliation. We do not read, analyse or retain email content that is not a financial document.

Sending: where you have granted it, Ava sends email from your connected inbox for one purpose: bookkeeping correspondence on your behalf — asking a supplier for a missing receipt or invoice, and replying in conversations you have asked her to handle. She never sends marketing or bulk mail, and every message she sends sits in your own Sent folder where you can see it.

Limited Use disclosure. Elastic Admin's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Google user data is never sold, never used for advertising, and never used to train generalised artificial-intelligence or machine-learning models.

How it is stored and secured

  • Extracted document details and filed copies of financial documents are stored in our database and file storage (Supabase, hosted on AWS, ap-southeast-1 region), encrypted at rest and in transit.
  • Google OAuth tokens are stored server-side only and are never exposed to browsers or third parties.
  • Automated processing uses vetted service providers (e.g. Anthropic for document reading) under terms that prohibit training on your data.

Who can see it

Access is limited to Elastic Admin's bookkeeping staff, strictly for preparing your own business's books. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for their own purposes. A human bookkeeper — never the application — commits entries in your accounting system.

Retention and deletion

Data is kept while your account is active so your books retain their audit trail. When you disconnect an inbox we stop reading it immediately; you can also revoke access anytime at myaccount.google.com/permissions. On request, or when you leave the service, we delete stored mail-derived documents and revoke and delete the associated tokens. Email ava@elasticadmin.com for deletion requests — we action them within 30 days.
