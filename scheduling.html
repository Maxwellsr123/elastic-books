Morning, Max 👋

Jobs book themselves now — Ava triages your inbox, lines up the crew, and confirms the customer. You only price the quotes.

How this works
① A customer emails — Ava reads it and decides: real job, quote, or neither. Can’t tell? She asks the customer straight back.
② A job: Ava picks whoever’s free, asks them in your team Slack — their 👍 books it in Fergus and the customer gets confirmed by email, from your address, signed by Ava.
③ A quote: Ava drafts it in Fergus ready for you to price & send. She spots the send, checks in with the customer, and their yes books it like any job.
Every move lands in your Slack as it happens — step in only when Ava flags something.
